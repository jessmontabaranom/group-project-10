Our mission!


In Hull...

We work on a local scale with different RSPCA shelters, here are our main aims:


  1. To reduce strays on the streets and provide them with a loving and caring forever home

  2. To raise awareness about animal care and the responsibilites that come with having a pet

  3. To provide vaccinations and basic pet care and needs for those who can't afford it

  4. To provide puppy training classes and basic care for puppies and kittens


Around the UK and the rest of the globe...

We work around the UK and in countries around the world with various animal welfare charities, together we strive for:

  1. Being a positive influence for animal welfare change...
    Such as launching campaigns like launcing an inquiry into the Breed Specific Legislation - which is a law that causes many innocent dogs to suffer and die

  2. Microchipping pets nationally with specially converted vans that drive from city to city

  3. Giving a portion of our donations to other animal welfare charities within the UK and abroad

  4. Contributing to shutting down research facilities where animals are used as test subjects



Thank you for your support and donations and making these aims possible to achieve! We couldn't do it without you.
Many thanks from the whole team and from the animals too!
