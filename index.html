Fundraising events:

  • Our next event will be a dog walk on the Yorkshire Moors - the Gallon Walk! Come and join us for a day of fun walking from pub to pub and making new friends! For every pub visited, £2 (or a larger amount, your choice) will be donated to the pet shelter! Hope to see you all there next month!


  • Our latest fundraising event was a cake bake at the RSPCA branch in Hull! We had many little bakers come and support our cause and made a profit of over £100 that day! Many thanks to everyone who bake and who bought them too!
